Golden Retriever

This is a Golden Retriever:

There are some characteristics of Golden Retrievers:

  1. Height: 21.5-24 inches
  2. Weight: 55-75 pounds
  3. Life span: 10-12 years
  4. Colors: cream gold / yellow

About Golden Retriever Temperament:

  • In general, golden retrievers are outgoing, playful, intelligent, friendly and gentle
  • They are bred to work with people and are eager to please their owners
